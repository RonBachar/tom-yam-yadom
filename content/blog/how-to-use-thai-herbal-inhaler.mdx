# How to Use Your Thai Herbal Inhaler

Getting the most out of your Smiling Tiger inhaler takes about thirty seconds to learn and a lifetime to enjoy. The technique is simple, but a few small details make a real difference in the experience. Here is exactly how to do it.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these steps each time you use your inhaler:

1. Remove the cap. Unscrew or lift the cap fully before bringing the inhaler near your nose.
2. Position it below one nostril. Hold the inhaler about half an inch to one inch (one to two centimeters) below your nostril. Do not insert it into your nose.
3. Close the other nostril. Gently press one finger against the nostril you are not using. This directs the airflow and gives you a fuller, more focused aromatic experience.
4. Breathe in slowly and gently. Take one to three calm, easy breaths through your nose. There is no need to inhale hard. A slow, relaxed breath carries the aroma much more effectively than a sharp sniff.
5. Switch to the other nostril. Repeat the same steps on the other side.
6. Replace the cap securely. Close the cap tightly after each use to preserve the aroma and extend the life of your inhaler.

That is the complete technique. Simple, portable, and ready whenever you need a reset.

## A Few Tips for the Best Experience

**Use it with intention.** The inhaler works best as a small ritual rather than a quick sniff. Pause for a moment, breathe slowly, and let the aromatics do their thing.

**Pair it with slow breathing.** For a deeper reset, try inhaling for a count of four and exhaling for a count of six. The combination of slow breath and aromatic scent is noticeably more grounding than either alone.

**Keep the cap on between uses.** The botanicals inside are concentrated and sealed. Leaving the cap off shortens the life of your inhaler faster than anything else.

**Use it as often as you like.** There is no dose to manage here. Reach for it whenever your attention drifts, your energy dips, or you just want a moment of freshness.
