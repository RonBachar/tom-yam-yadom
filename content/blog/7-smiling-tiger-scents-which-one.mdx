One of the first questions people ask when they discover [Tom Yam Yadom](/blog/what-is-yadom-thai-herbal-inhaler) is simple: which scent do I get? We make seven distinct Smiling Tiger profiles, each built for a different mood and moment.

This guide walks through all seven, what each one feels like, and when to reach for it. Whether you're after focus at your desk, grounding in a crowd, or a lift before you compete, there's a profile here with your name on it.

## How to Think About Choosing

Before the individual profiles, a simple way to narrow it down. Ask yourself what you most want from a single slow breath. Do you want to come up (energy, alertness, a lift)? Do you want to come down (calm, grounding, a settle)? Or do you want to lock in (sharp, clear focus)?

Here's the full lineup.

## The 7 Profiles

### Compassion

Compassion is the warm, open-hearted profile, soft and rounded rather than sharp. It's the one to reach for in reflective moments: when you want to feel settled, kind toward yourself, and a little more present with the people around you. A gentle everyday companion rather than a jolt. [Explore Compassion here](/products/compassion).

### Vitality

Vitality is bright, lively, and energizing, your come-alive profile. It's built for the moments you need to shake off sluggishness and find some spark: the start of a big day, the afternoon slump, or the walk into a festival. If you want a clean lift without caffeine, this is a natural pick. It earns its place in any [festival kit](/blog/best-festival-essential-herbal-inhaler). [Explore Vitality here](/products/vitality).

### Radiance

Radiance is the uplifting, feel-good profile, sunny and expansive. Where Vitality is about raw energy, Radiance is about mood: that warm, glowing, things-are-good feeling. It shines in peak moments you want to lean into, like the headliner set or a great day out with friends. [Explore Radiance here](/products/radiance).

### Balance

Balance is the grounded, centered profile, steady and even. It's the one for staying composed: deep focused work, a stressful day that needs leveling out, or any moment you want to feel solid rather than scattered. It's a favorite for calm, sustained concentration, which is why it features in our guide to the [best natural focus inhaler](/blog/best-natural-inhaler-for-focus). [Explore Balance here](/products/balance).

### Power

Power is the bold, intense, get-ready profile, the most assertive of the seven. It's made for locking in before big efforts: training, competition, or anything you need to psych up for. It's a natural fit for combat athletes preparing to perform, as we cover in our piece on [how Muay Thai fighters use herbal inhalers](/blog/muay-thai-herbal-inhaler-recovery-focus). [Explore Power here](/products/power).

### Clarity

Clarity is the sharp, cool, crystal-clear profile, the most focus-forward scent we make. Bright and penetrating, it's the one to reach for when you need to think fast and stay alert: deep work, study sessions, long drives, anything that demands a quick, clean mind. [Explore Clarity here](/products/clarity).

### Serenity

Serenity is the calm, grounding, come-down profile, the gentlest reset of the seven. It's made for settling: easing off after intense effort, steadying yourself when a crowd gets to be too much, or replacing the restless energy of a habit you're trying to quit. It's a recurring recommendation in both our [quit-smoking guide](/blog/quitting-smoking-inhaler-beats-fidgeting) and for [post-training recovery](/blog/muay-thai-herbal-inhaler-recovery-focus). [Explore Serenity here](/products/serenity).

## Quick Picks by Goal

If you'd rather just be pointed in a direction:

- **For sharp focus at work:** Clarity, or Balance if you prefer calm focus over sharp focus.
- **For energy and a lift:** Vitality, with Radiance for the feel-good peak moments.
- **For calm and grounding:** Serenity, with Balance for a steadier everyday center.
- **For training and competition:** Power to get up, Serenity to come down afterward.
- **For festivals:** Vitality and Radiance to lift, Serenity to land.
- **For quitting a habit:** Serenity and Balance for the restless moments.
- **For everyday warmth:** Compassion.
