# How to Use Crown Blend Oil

Crown Blend is a traditional Thai herbal oil, richer and more versatile than an inhaler. You can breathe it, apply it, and use it across a wider range of everyday moments. Here is a complete guide to getting the most out of every drop.

If you want to understand where this tradition comes from first, start with our guide to [what yadom is and its history](/blog/what-is-yadom-thai-herbal-inhaler).

## Ways to Use Crown Blend Oil

Crown Blend works in several different ways. Use whichever method fits the moment.

### Aromatic Inhalation

Hold the open bottle a short distance below your nose and take one to three slow, gentle breaths. This is the most direct way to experience the full aromatic profile of the blend.

### On a Tissue or Cloth

Place one to two drops on a tissue or cloth and inhale the scent from it. This method lets the aroma disperse slightly, which some people prefer for a softer, more ambient experience.

### On the Nostrils

Dab a very small amount on the outside of your nostrils, just beneath them, and breathe normally. The warmth of your skin carries the scent steadily for an extended aromatic effect.

### For Sore Muscles or Joints

Apply two to three drops to the area and massage gently until absorbed. The warming botanicals in the blend make it a natural choice for tired muscles after training or a long day.

### For Headaches

Apply one to two drops to your temples, your forehead, or the back of your neck and massage gently. Keep the oil away from your eyes.

### For Insect Bites

Apply a small drop directly to the bite and rub it in gently.

## After Each Use

Wash your hands after application unless your hands were the area being treated. Close the cap tightly after every use and store the bottle in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

## Important Warnings

For external use only. Do not swallow. Avoid contact with the eyes, mouth, broken skin, and the inside of the nose. If irritation occurs, discontinue use.
